Spam Filter

Filtering the Spam Messages in Your Emails is An Essential Part of the work of Every Good Email Account Provider. This Model Here is Doing Exactly This - It Separates Spam from non-Spam Messages. It's trained on old data with SMS spam messages but it still works relatively well in general.

If You Have Ever Been Working with Standard Machine Learning models You have Most Certainly Heard of an Algorithm Called Support Vector Machines. This is a very Heavyweight, Slow but Extremely Powerful Algorithm used Mostly for solving problems that do not have large datasets.



I didn't start this Project with the Idea to train SVM. It just Produced Better than the other models I've tried.

When it Comes to Spam Filtering there are two very Different Points of View - One that Prefers to Get More Spam Messages but Almost Never Miss an Import One by Putting it in 'Spam' and the Other that would Gladly Open More Often 'Spam' but Not Letting Any Garbage in the Main Section.

In My Research I was Able to Achieve Better Results with Different algorithms in Terms of Not Letting Any Spam or Never Putting a Real Message in 'Spam section'. But Overall Accuracy of the SVM was the Best. It Had 99% Accuracy on Training Data and 98.8% Accuracy on New Data. It Was The Most Balanced Model between the Two Points of View. Overall it was the best.
